# Omni, Powered by Engine

Through the Omni API, companies can integrate Engine's lodging inventory directly into their own products.
While Engine's platform also offers flights and rental cars, our API currently focuses exclusively on lodging.

The Omni API is ideal for businesses looking to:

* **Provide travel to their users as a first-time offering**
* **Integrate Omni as a new lodging partner** into their existing travel booking platform.
* **Build a brand-new travel product** that leverages Omni's extensive lodging inventory.

To begin integrating, please [review our documentation](https://engine-public.github.io/engine-partner-api).
Once you’re ready, please complete this [form](https://omni.engine.com/#contact) and contact [omni-partnerships@engine.com](mailto:omni-partnerships@engine.com) to discuss a partnership agreement, gain access to your sandbox environment, and start the development process.

_Note: Engine reviews requests as they are submitted, and our team will work with you to align on development timelines._
